Our Space

Not just a café.
A whole other world.

Tucked behind bamboo walls and a pair of coconut palms, Aloha Café is Vijayawada's most unhurried address. A converted villa with an open courtyard, a garden corridor lit after dark, and a verandah that seems to slow time on its own.

Whether you arrive with a laptop or someone worth talking to, the space accommodates both — without trying too hard.
